The Material

Found Objects uses an open source design tool to create new material from the leftover space of CNC sheets. The shape of each piece of material is generated to fill-in available sheet-space. This means that the exact shape is unique, while the general forms generated can be controlled: from organic and curvy to angular and geometric.

Order Here!

While material pieces are unique, there are some charachteristics that can be chosen, such as the length and maximum width of the pieces. It's possible to specifiy a particualr material and thickness, or to work with whatever materials are in currently in production by choosing 'Material X' or 'Any Thickness'. The more flexible you are in your selection, the greater chance there is that your material will be found!
